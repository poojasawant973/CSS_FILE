Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Tomato Slices
  • Butter
  • Bread Slices

Instructions

1. Place bacon in a skillet and cook until it's crispy.

2. Spread butter on one side of each bread slice.

3. On the unbuttered side of a bread slice, layer cheddar cheese, cooked bacon, and tomato slices.

4. Top with another bread slice, buttered side up.

5. Heat a skillet over medium-low heat and cook the sandwich until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 4 minutes per side.
